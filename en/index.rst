Topo GPS manual iOS
===================

The manual of the topographic maps navigation app `Topo GPS <https://www.topo-gps.com/download/>`_ for iOS (iPhone/iPad).

- Did you buy a new iPhone or iPad? Please `encrypt the backup of your old device in iTunes <http://support.apple.com/kb/HT4946>`_ and then install this backup on your new device. In this way the maps you bought remain accessible.
